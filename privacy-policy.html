1. Introduction

Olow Technologies, Inc. ("Olow," "we," "us," or "our") provides file-sharing applications for Android and Desktop. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our apps, websites, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using Olow, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to deliver a fast, reliable sharing experience:

Information you provide

  • Account details such as your name and email address when you register.
  • Communications you send us, including support requests and feedback.
  • Optional profile preferences and settings you configure in the app.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information such as model, operating system version, and app version.
  • Usage data such as transfer counts, file sizes, and feature interactions.
  • Approximate location derived from your IP address for security and regional routing.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Services.
  • Process transfers and deliver your files to intended recipients.
  • Secure accounts, detect abuse, and prevent fraud.
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • Send important service notices and, with consent, product updates.

We do not sell your personal information, and we never use the contents of your files for advertising.

4. Your Files

Files you transfer through Olow are encrypted in transit and at rest. We treat the contents of your files as private and access them only when strictly necessary to operate the Services, comply with the law, or with your explicit permission.

You control how long your files remain available through link expiry settings, and you can delete files and revoke links at any time.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal compliance where required by applicable law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you.
  • With your consent for any other purpose disclosed at the time of collection.

6. Data Security

We apply industry-standard safeguards including encryption, access controls, and continuous monitoring to protect your information. While no system is perfectly secure, we work continually to protect your data and to address vulnerabilities promptly.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Transferred files are retained according to your link and expiry settings.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your information.
  • Request a portable copy of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@olow.io.

9. Cookies

Our websites use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

10. Children's Privacy

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
