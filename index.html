How It Works

Transfer files in seconds

Transfer files in seconds with a simple and secure process.

STEP 01

Open OLOWShare

Launch OLOWShare on the sender device and tap the Send Files option. This is the starting point for transferring photos, videos, apps, documents, contacts, music, and other files to another device.

STEP 02

Select Files

Browse your device and choose everything you want to share. OLOWShare allows you to quickly select multiple files at once, making it easy to transfer large collections in a single session.

STEP 03

Generate QR Code

Once your files are selected, OLOWShare instantly generates a secure QR code. This QR code creates a fast connection between devices without requiring complicated setup steps.

STEP 04

Open Receiver Device

On the receiving device, open OLOWShare and choose the option to receive files. The receiver is now ready to establish a connection with the sender device.

STEP 05

Scan & Connect

Use the receiver device to scan the QR code displayed on the sender phone. Within seconds, both devices recognize each other and establish a secure connection.

STEP 06

Files Sent

The selected files begin transferring immediately. OLOWShare uses high-speed local connections to move files quickly without unnecessary delays or interruptions.

STEP 07

Files Received

The transfer is completed successfully and all selected files are available on the receiving device. Photos, videos, documents, and other content arrive ready to use instantly.

STEP 01

Open WebShare

Open OLOWShare on the sender phone and tap WebShare. This starts the browser-based sharing process and prepares the phone to create a connection for nearby devices.

STEP 02

Choose Hotspot Mode

Select Hotspot Mode when you want to share files without using internet data. The receiver connects directly to the sender phone’s hotspot for a fast local transfer.

STEP 03

Join The Network

On the receiving device, join the hotspot network created by the sender phone. This connects both devices to the same local transfer environment.

STEP 04

Open The URL

Open the WebShare URL in the receiver’s browser. This allows the receiver device to access the sender phone through a simple web connection.

STEP 05

Check WiFi Connection

Confirm that the receiving device is connected to the correct hotspot network before continuing. This helps make sure the WebShare connection works smoothly.

STEP 06

Request Permission

The sender phone receives a connection request from the receiver. This step keeps the transfer controlled and prevents unknown devices from connecting without approval.

STEP 07

Allow Access

Approve the request on the sender phone. Once permission is granted, the receiving device can connect securely and prepare to receive files.

STEP 08

View Connected Devices

The receiver device appears in the connected devices list, confirming that the WebShare connection is active and ready for file transfer.

STEP 09

Sender Connected

The sender phone confirms that the connection has been established successfully and files can now be shared through WebShare.

STEP 10

Receiver Connected

The receiver device also confirms the successful connection, showing that both sides are paired and ready to transfer files.

STEP 11

Files Sent Successfully

The sender selects and sends the files. OLOWShare completes the transfer through the hotspot connection, making it easy to share without internet data.

Open WebShare
